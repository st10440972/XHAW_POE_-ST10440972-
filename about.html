About Skill Grow Academy

Skill Grow Academy is a community-first learning centre focused on older adults. We offer accessible, easy to follow courses that help learners gain digital, practical and wellness skills.

Why we exist

To give elderly learners the opportunity to stay active, learn new skills and feel more confident using technology and everyday systems.
